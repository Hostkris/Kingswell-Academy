About Kingswell Academy

Practical learning without burnout.

Kingswell Academy gives adults and self-learners a structured path for rebuilding academic confidence and gaining usable knowledge.

School mission

Kingswell Academy is a practical online school for adults and self-learners.

Kingswell Academy is a practical online school for adults and self-learners who want to rebuild their academic foundation, improve comprehension, and gain real-world knowledge through a structured 18-month learning roadmap. The platform combines short daily lessons, syllabus calendars, course tracking, study timers, portfolio notes, and applied projects to help users become more skilled, confident, and well-rounded without burnout.

Creator's note: I am the creator of this academy, Kistidis Cardy.

What learners build

  • Stronger reading, writing, math, science, research, psychology, business, and design foundations.
  • Daily study habits supported by calendars, timers, tracking, and saved notes.
  • Portfolio proof through applied projects, corrections, reflections, and mastery evidence.

How the academy works

  • Follow an 18-month roadmap with Basic, Intermediate, and Advanced learning levels.
  • Use short lessons and practice routines to keep progress steady and manageable.
  • Connect school skills to real-world decisions, projects, communication, and confidence.

Install & Deployment

GitHub upload

  1. Upload the contents of this folder to the repository root.
  2. Keep index.html, styles.css, data.js, app.js, manifest.webmanifest, service-worker.js, and assets/ at the root.
  3. Go to Settings → Pages → Deploy from branch → main → root.
  4. Open the GitHub Pages link after it publishes.

Production note

This is a strong static front-end school portal. For real enrolled students, payments, secure accounts, teacher dashboards, anti-cheat grading, certificates, and cloud progress sync need a backend.

GitHub Pages can host the school interface. It cannot securely hold private student records by itself.
