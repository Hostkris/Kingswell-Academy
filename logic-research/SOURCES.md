# Sources and Curriculum Notes

## Course Text Categories

This platform is designed to use instructor-selected books as curriculum foundations.

## Uploaded/Provided Book Concepts

- The Overthinker's Guide to Making Decisions — used for original lessons about decision-making, overthinking, root decisions, fear-based reasoning, and taking small next steps.
- Uncommon Sense Teaching — used for learning strategies, memory, practice, and study structure.
- The Concise Laws of Human Nature — used for human behavior, motives, bias, social reasoning, and case analysis.
- The Parasitic Mind — used for idea analysis, persuasion, ideology, belief systems, and argument critique.

## Required Academic Additions

To make the course academically complete, add:

- One formal logic textbook or open logic source
- One research-writing or research-methods textbook
- Citation guide such as APA, MLA, or Chicago depending on the school style

## Copyright Rule

Do not copy copyrighted book text into the website unless permission or license allows it.

Use:

- Original summaries
- Original examples
- Original practice questions
- Original answer explanations
- Original worksheets
- Proper citations in teacher-facing source notes

The books guide the curriculum. The website teaches through original instruction and assessment.
