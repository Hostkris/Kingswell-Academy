Self-Check Rubric

  • Concept understanding: Mastery — Explains scientific method and observation clearly in plain language. Developing — Names the topic but cannot explain how it works.
  • Application: Mastery — Uses the skill correctly in testing an idea with observation, data, and cause/effect. Developing — Only repeats the classroom example or guesses.
  • Correction: Mastery — Finds one error, fixes it, and explains why the new answer is stronger. Developing — Changes the answer without explaining the reason.
  • Portfolio proof: Mastery — Submits a complete mini investigation log with reflection. Developing — Submits incomplete proof or a screenshot with no thinking shown.
